## Overview
This page serves as the index for my AWS notes, which I am gradually migrating over from Notion.

These notes were originally taken while studying for the **AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Associate** (SAA-C02) certification examination, and are being cleaned up into a more structured and consistent format as I move them here.

Rather than trying to replicate AWS documentation, my goal with these notes is to focus on:
- core concepts in a way that is easy to understand,
- service-to-service distinctions,
- common architectural trade-offs for future reference,
- and of course, exam-relevant details.
